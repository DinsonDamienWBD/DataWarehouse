<objective>
Create VdeResizeEngine for online volume grow operations.

Purpose: Enable VDE volumes to grow online without unmounting. The engine extends the allocation bitmap, creates new allocation groups for added space, updates TotalBlocks/FreeBlocks in the superblock atomically, and journals the entire operation via OPJR for crash recovery. Readers continue unblocked; writers queue behind a lightweight barrier (<1ms) only during the atomic superblock update.

Output: VdeResizeEngine class with GrowAsync method, plus comprehensive TDD tests.
</objective>
